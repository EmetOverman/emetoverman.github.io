title: The Point of a Small Website
description: Why this site starts with a small, durable structure.
---

A personal site does not need much to be useful. It needs a stable URL, a
clear reading experience, and an easy publishing path.

That is the point of this setup. Everything important is plain text, the
navigation is shallow, and the homepage doubles as an archive instead of trying
to be a full product landing page.

The result is easier to maintain, easier to extend, and easier to keep
current.
