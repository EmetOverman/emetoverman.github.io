The site now runs on Jekyll and keeps the original structure intentionally
small: a homepage archive, a couple of permanent pages, and Markdown-first
content that can be maintained directly in the repository.

The design comes from Andrew Hwan Park's `brutalist-blog` theme, adapted here as
repo-owned layouts, includes, and styles so the site can be customized without
depending on an external theme package.

From here the highest-value edits are simple:

1. Replace the starter copy on the About and Portfolio pages.
2. Add real posts under `_posts/`.
3. Fill in any additional footer links or analytics details in `_config.yml`.
