This site is a small, durable home for writing, project notes, and selected
work.

### What lives here

- Short essays and working notes
- Projects that are still evolving
- A minimal archive that stays readable on desktop and mobile

### Why this format

The structure is intentionally simple: plain Markdown, a few permanent pages,
and just enough styling to make long-form writing pleasant to read in both
light and dark mode.
